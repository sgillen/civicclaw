Three ways to use this

Read & ask

Read your district's weekly digest right here. Want to ask follow-up questions? Copy the report as a prompt and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI chat. Free, no account needed.

Automated digests

Install the sf-civic-digest skill on Claude Code and get personalized weekly reports delivered automatically. Set your district, your streets, what you care about.

Build your own

Fork the repo and adapt it for your city. The scripts, style guide, and data pipeline are all open source. SF's data sources are well-documented — yours can be too.
