## Potential Actions

### 🎯 Featured: Broderick Street Metering (Wiggle Route)

**What's happening:** SFMTA will vote Thursday on installing meters along Broderick between Fell and Oak. Item 11 on the Engineering Hearing agenda.

**Why you'd care:** This is the Wiggle — your main bike route from the Panhandle to Lower Haight. Metering changes traffic patterns. More turnover could mean more cars circling for spots, or it could mean less congestion from cars camping in free spaces. The city says it's about "parking availability and turnover."
