## Potential Actions

### Featured: Lucky Supermarket — Board of Supervisors, Tue 4/7, 2 PM

**What's happening:** Save Mart plans to close the Lucky at 1750 Fulton on September 11. The Board votes Tuesday on a non-binding resolution urging them to reconsider.

**Why you'd care:** This is the full-service grocery for Western Addition. Without a car, the alternatives are a serious trek. It's a food access issue for seniors and low-income residents in the surrounding blocks.

**What you can do:** Public comment at Tuesday's Board meeting (2 PM, City Hall Room 250). If you shop here, saying so matters. Email Supervisor Mahmood's office: mahmoodstaff@sfgov.org.
