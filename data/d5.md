## Potential Actions

### Featured: Lucky Supermarket — Board of Supervisors, Tue 4/7, 2 PM

**What's happening:** Save Mart has announced plans to close the Lucky Supermarket at 1750 Fulton Street on September 11. The Board will vote Tuesday on a resolution urging Save Mart to reverse the decision. This is the full-service grocery store serving the heart of Western Addition — the alternative options are a hike for residents without cars.

**Why you'd care:** Grocery access in Western Addition is already thin. Losing 1750 Fulton means seniors and low-income residents in the surrounding blocks lose walkable access to a full-service store. This is a food access issue, not just a commercial lease story.

**What you can do:** The resolution is non-binding — it asks Save Mart to reconsider, it doesn't force anything. But public comment at Tuesday's Board meeting (2 PM, City Hall Room 250) puts the grocery access issue on the record. If you shop at this Lucky, saying so matters. You can also email Supervisor Mahmood's office: mahmoodstaff@sfgov.org.
