## Potential Actions

### Featured: Landmark Designations Hearing — Three D9 Properties

**What's happening:** Land Use and Transportation Committee hears 14 landmark designations on Monday, April 6 at 1:30 PM. Three are in D9: the Mission Folk Victorian Home at 361 San Jose Ave (between 25th and 26th), the former Engine Company No. 13 firehouse at 1458 Valencia St (between 25th and 26th), and St. Matthew's Church at 3281 16th St (between Dolores and Guerrero). Landmark status under Article 10 adds preservation constraints that make demolition or major alteration significantly harder.

**Why you'd care:** If you live near these properties, landmark status shapes what can and can't happen to the buildings long-term. If you care about the balance between preservation and new development in the Mission corridor, this is a direct policy lever.

**What you can do:** Public comment is open at the hearing — Land Use Committee, Mon 4/6, 1:30 PM, City Hall Room 263. You can also email the committee clerk before the hearing.
