The housing story in District 9 is less about whether projects can win approvals than whether they can convert those approvals into actual permits, financing, and construction. 2675 FOLSOM ST 94110 (237 units) is closed - approval ltr issued; 1458 SAN BRUNO AVE 94110 (231 units) is under review; 1500 15TH ST 94103 (190 units) is closed. Under the citywide benchmark, a one-year post-approval pause can still be normal for market-rate projects, but multi-year drift or outright cancellations are the real warning signs.
