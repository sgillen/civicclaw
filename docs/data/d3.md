The housing story in District 3 is less about whether projects can win approvals than whether they can convert those approvals into actual permits, financing, and construction. 1123 SUTTER ST 94109 (222 units) is closed - approval ltr issued; 436 GEARY ST 94102 (122 units) is closed; 1462 PINE ST 94109 (104 units) is submitted. Under the citywide benchmark, a one-year post-approval pause can still be normal for market-rate projects, but multi-year drift or outright cancellations are the real warning signs.
