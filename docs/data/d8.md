The housing story in District 8 is less about whether projects can win approvals than whether they can convert those approvals into actual permits, financing, and construction. 1965 MARKET ST 94103 (218 units) is under review; 1965 MARKET ST 94103 (96 units) is closed; 2134 MARKET ST 94114 (88 units) is on hold. Under the citywide benchmark, a one-year post-approval pause can still be normal for market-rate projects, but multi-year drift or outright cancellations are the real warning signs.
