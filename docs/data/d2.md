## Housing

The housing story in District 2 is less about whether projects can win approvals than whether they can convert those approvals into actual permits, financing, and construction. 15 MARINA BLVD 94123 (790 units) is under review; 1015 VAN NESS AVE 94109 (124 units) is closed - approval ltr issued; 3300 GEARY BLVD 94118 (62 units) is submitted. Under the citywide benchmark, a one-year post-approval pause can still be normal for market-rate projects, but multi-year drift or outright cancellations are the real warning signs.
