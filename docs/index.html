This is a public frontend for my personal CivicClaw agent. Every week it scrapes 22 government data sources and 5 local newsrooms -Board of Supervisors agendas, building permits, housing pipeline, 311 data, ethics filings, meeting transcripts, and more. It surfaces things I care about and tries to find actionable items for me. I really do think this works best as an agent -it knows my particular interests and I can ask it to follow up on things or do more research for me. But this website hopefully gives you a taste.
